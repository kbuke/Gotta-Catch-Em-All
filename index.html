Who's That Pokemon?

Team Rocket have attacked the Kanto Region. It's up to you and your team of three Pokemon to take them on and save the day. Assemble your team by:
  • Placing your cursor over the Pokemon Card to reveal which Pokemon lies underneath.
  • When you see a Pokemon you like drag its image into one of the containers on the right hand side.
  • There are only three spots, so if you want to change your team, drag the image of the Pokemon you want to remove out of the container, and then drag in the new one
  • Filter Pokemon by their types with the dropdown menu on the left.
  • Or, if you know the name of the Pokemon you want begin typing it in the text bar on the right and it will appear.
